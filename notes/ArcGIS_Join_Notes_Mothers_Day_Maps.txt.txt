ArcGIS Join Notes
Paid Maternity Leave Around the World

Main cleaned file:
WBL2026_Maternity_Leave_ArcGIS.csv

Source:
World Bank, Women, Business and the Law 2026, Parenthood indicators.

Primary join field:
ISO_A3

Main mapped field:
MAT_CLASS_WEEKS

Mapped classes:
0 weeks
1–13 weeks
14–26 weeks
26–51 weeks
52+ weeks

Notes:
The cleaned CSV was joined to a world countries boundary layer using ISO_A3 country/economy codes. The final map was symbolized by maternity leave class and published as both a static infographic and an interactive ArcGIS Online map.

Limitations:
This map shows paid maternity leave available to mothers. It does not represent total parental leave, unpaid leave, wage replacement rates, eligibility rules, or actual uptake.
