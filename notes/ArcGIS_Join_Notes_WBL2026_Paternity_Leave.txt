Paid Paternity Leave Around the World

Main cleaned file:
WBL2026_Paternity_Leave_ArcGIS.csv

Source:
World Bank, Women, Business and the Law 2026, Parenthood indicators.

Primary join field:
ISO_A3

Main mapped field:
PAT_CLASS_WEEKS

Mapped classes:
0 weeks
<1 week
1 week
2–3 weeks
4+ weeks

Notes:
The cleaned CSV was derived from the WBL 2026 Parenthood indicators and prepared for joining to a world countries boundary layer using ISO_A3 country/economy codes.

Limitations:
This map shows paid leave available to fathers. It does not represent total parental leave, unpaid leave, wage replacement rates, eligibility rules, or actual uptake.
